DHSK starts first responder course.

In a bid to help save more lives, directorate of health services Kashmir (DHSK) is starting a first responder course (FRC) for masses under a Union ministry of health initiative. FRC has been introduced by the MoHFW to empower citizens to be the first person to provide pre-hospital care. It will impart first aid and primary care skills that can save lives in case of an emergency. FRC will be launched in March 2018.
